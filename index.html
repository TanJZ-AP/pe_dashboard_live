DG Apr 11 Oddle net was $1,563, nearly 6× its next highest day (Thu $253) and exceeded DG Grab for the same day ($755). Deliveroo recorded $0 for the 7th consecutive week.

Last week Mon–Fri was 62% with Sat+Sun at 38% (though LW had the GF holiday). The weekend surge is structural — both outlets are converting strong evening dining demand on weekends. Prioritise weekend staffing, upsell readiness, and kitchen throughput planning ahead of the coming weeks.
